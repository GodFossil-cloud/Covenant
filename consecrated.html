Your oath has been spoken. Your signature has been placed. You are now initiated.

By word and hand, you have bound yourself to the Covenant. What was threshold is now path. What was potential is now commitment. You are no longer on the outside looking in. You are within, and you carry the obligation of your oath forward into every breath, every choice, every moment.

This is not the end of initiation. This is the beginning of your initiated life.

Your Path Forward

  1. Return daily to your practices; they remain non‑negotiable.
  2. When difficulty arises, remember your oath and return to practice.
  3. Track your progress: where you succeed, where you fail, where you grow.
  4. At regular intervals, return to the Covenant and re‑read it with new eyes.
  5. Share this tradition with no one unless you are explicitly asked to do so.
  6. Your initiated status is permanent; the oath you have spoken cannot be unspoken.

The threshold has closed behind you. The path extends forward into the life you are now building with intention and presence.
