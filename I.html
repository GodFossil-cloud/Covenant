Article Ⅰ: Of Sacred Silence

✦ ✧ ✦

Of Sacred Silence[8]The principle that all Covenant matters remain veiled from profane sight.

§.Ⅰ All matters pertaining to this Covenant[2]A sacred, binding agreement between the Lord and the Faithful.—the bond between Lord and Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond., commands[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord. given, corrections administered, and private discourse—shall remain veiled from profane sight.
§.Ⅱ Neither party shall reveal the nature, terms, or particulars herein to any soul unbidden, save where earthly law compels disclosure or both consent.
§.Ⅲ What is consecrated[6]Sacred blessing and binding force that seals this Covenant. between Lord and Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. abides in sacred confidence, for to profane the mystery of this bond is to desecrate its very foundation and violate the trust upon which all rests.
§.Ⅳ The sanctity of this silence protects the flame that burns between them, shielding it from winds of common judgment and the gaze of those who cannot comprehend the sacred order freely chosen and faithfully maintained.
§.Ⅴ Violation of Sacred Silence[8]The principle that all Covenant matters remain veiled from profane sight. constitutes breach[9]A violation of Covenant terms and sacred obligations. of fundamental trust and shall be addressed under the provisions of Article Ⅹ.
