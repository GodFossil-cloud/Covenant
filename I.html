Article ‚Ö†: Of Sacred Silence

Article I establishes Sacred Silence as the protective veil over all Covenant matters. This principle guards the bond from external judgment, maintaining the privacy necessary for a sacred relationship to flourish without profane interference.

The Article frames disclosure as desecration and violation of fundamental trust. Only legal compulsion or mutual consent permits revelation, preserving the mystery and sanctity that define this bond.
