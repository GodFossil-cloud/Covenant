Invocation and Preamble

In the presence of the sacred bond between Lord†The ordained authority who commands, guides, and protects the Faithful. and Faithful‡The devoted servant who obeys, serves, and honors the Lord., this Covenant is established.

This document serves as the foundation of our sacred structure—a binding agreement that defines the hierarchy, duties, and mutual devotion between Lord and Faithful. It is not merely a set of rules, but a testament to the trust, reverence, and commitment that binds us together.

By entering into this Covenant, both parties acknowledge the gravity of their roles and pledge to uphold the principles herein with integrity, honor, and unwavering dedication.

✦ ✧ ✦

Doctrinal Foundation

This Covenant is rooted in the following core principles:

I. Hierarchy and Authority: The Lord holds supreme authority within the bounds of this Covenant. The Faithful submits willingly and completely to this authority.
II. Mutual Devotion: Both Lord and Faithful are bound by mutual care, respect, and devotion. The Lord guides and protects; the Faithful obeys and serves.
III. Sacred Trust: This bond is built on trust. The Lord trusts the Faithful to obey; the Faithful trusts the Lord to lead with wisdom and care.
IV. Ceremonial Reverence: Rituals, titles, and forms of address are not mere formalities but sacred expressions of the hierarchy and devotion that define this relationship.
✦ ✧ ✦

Declaration of Intent

The Lord and Faithful hereby declare their intent to enter into this Covenant freely, knowingly, and with full understanding of its terms. This is a sacred commitment, and both parties pledge to honor it with reverence and integrity.

✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅰ: Sacred Silence

The Sacred Silence§A state of total obedience in which the Faithful ceases speech, awaiting the Lord's permission to speak. is invoked when the Lord commands it. During this state:

§1. The Faithful shall cease all speech immediately upon the Lord's command.
§2. The Faithful shall remain silent until the Lord grants explicit permission to speak.
§3. The Sacred Silence may be invoked for disciplinary purposes, for ceremonial reverence, or at the Lord's discretion.
§4. Violation of the Sacred Silence is a grave transgression and shall be addressed accordingly.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅱ: Titles and Forms of Address

Titles and forms of address are sacred expressions of the hierarchy between Lord and Faithful.

§1. The Faithful shall address the Lord as "Lord" or by any other title the Lord designates.
§2. The Lord may address the Faithful as "Faithful," "servant," or by any other designation the Lord chooses.
§3. The Faithful shall not use the Lord's common name unless explicitly permitted to do so.
§4. Failure to use proper titles is a sign of disrespect and shall be corrected.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅲ: Duties of the Faithful

The Faithful pledges to uphold the following duties:

§1. Obedience: The Faithful shall obey all Commands¶Direct orders from the Lord that must be followed without question or delay. given by the Lord, promptly and without question.
§2. Service: The Faithful shall serve the Lord with devotion, humility, and care.
§3. Reverence: The Faithful shall honor the Lord through word, deed, and ritual observance.
§4. Transparency: The Faithful shall be honest and forthright with the Lord, concealing nothing of importance.
§5. Self-Improvement: The Faithful shall strive to better themselves in service to the Lord and to the Covenant.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅳ: Duties of the Lord

The Lord pledges to uphold the following duties:

§1. Guidance: The Lord shall guide the Faithful with wisdom, clarity, and care.
§2. Protection: The Lord shall protect the Faithful from harm, whether physical, emotional, or spiritual.
§3. Just Authority: The Lord shall exercise authority with fairness and integrity, never abusing the trust placed in them.
§4. Devotion: The Lord shall cherish and honor the Faithful, recognizing their service and devotion.
§5. Consistency: The Lord shall be consistent in their commands, corrections, and expectations.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅴ: Interdependence

The Lord and Faithful are bound together in sacred interdependence. Neither can fulfill their role without the other.

§1. The Lord's authority is meaningless without the Faithful's submission.
§2. The Faithful's service is purposeless without the Lord's guidance.
§3. Both parties shall recognize and honor this interdependence.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅵ: Ceremonial Boundaries

This Covenant defines ceremonial and symbolic boundaries, not physical ones. It governs:

§1. Forms of address and titles.
§2. Rituals and symbolic acts of devotion.
§3. The tone and manner of interaction between Lord and Faithful.
§4. It does not govern external matters, financial arrangements, or legal obligations, except where explicitly stated.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅶ: Interpretation and Clarification

The Lord holds the final authority in interpreting the terms of this Covenant.

§1. If any term or provision is unclear, the Lord's interpretation shall prevail.
§2. The Faithful may seek clarification but must accept the Lord's final judgment.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅷ: Commands and Compliance

Commands are sacred directives from the Lord to the Faithful.

§1. The Faithful shall obey all Commands without delay or hesitation.
§2. If a Command is unclear, the Faithful may respectfully ask for clarification before proceeding.
§3. Failure to obey a Command is a transgression and shall be addressed accordingly.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅸ: Appeals and Petitions

The Faithful may appeal to the Lord under certain circumstances.

§1. The Faithful may petition the Lord for mercy, clarification, or reconsideration.
§2. All petitions must be made respectfully and humbly.
§3. The Lord shall consider all petitions, but the final decision rests with the Lord.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅹ: Transgression and Correction

Transgressions against this Covenant shall be addressed through correction and discipline.

§1. The Lord shall correct transgressions with fairness and care.
§2. The Faithful shall accept correction with humility and gratitude.
§3. Repeated or severe transgressions may result in suspension or dissolution of the Covenant.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅺ: Amendments and Revisions

This Covenant may be amended or revised as needed.

§1. The Lord may propose amendments at any time.
§2. The Faithful may request amendments, subject to the Lord's approval.
§3. All amendments must be documented and agreed upon by both parties.
✦ ✧ ✦

Article Ⅻ: Suspension and Dissolution

This Covenant may be suspended or dissolved under certain conditions.

§1. Either party may request suspension of the Covenant for a specified period.
§2. Dissolution may occur by mutual agreement or in cases of severe breach.
§3. All suspension and dissolution shall be handled with dignity and respect.
✦ ✧ ✦

Glossary of Sacred Terms

Lord †
The ordained authority who commands, guides, and protects the Faithful. The Lord holds supreme authority within the bounds of this Covenant.
Faithful ‡
The devoted servant who obeys, serves, and honors the Lord. The Faithful submits willingly and completely to the Lord's authority.
Sacred Silence §
A state of total obedience in which the Faithful ceases speech, awaiting the Lord's permission to speak. Invoked at the Lord's command.
Command ¶
A direct order from the Lord that must be followed without question or delay. Commands are sacred directives within the Covenant.
Transgression **
A violation of the terms, duties, or spirit of this Covenant. Transgressions are addressed through correction and discipline.
Petition ††
A respectful request from the Faithful to the Lord for mercy, clarification, or reconsideration of a command or decision.
