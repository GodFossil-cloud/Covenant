Article Ⅸ: Of Appeals and Resolution

✦ ✧ ✦

Of Appeals and Resolution

§.Ⅰ All disputes shall be addressed promptly ere discord take root.
§.Ⅱ The Lord holds final authority in interpretation and arbitration. His determinations stand unless mercy or reflection cause Him to revise them.
§.Ⅲ Formal Appeals Process
Ⓐ Appeals shall be concise and composed, opening with “This is a petition for reconsideration, not defiance.”
Ⓑ Permissible Grounds: Misinterpretation, impossibility, conflict of decrees, unforeseen consequence. Not permitted: preference, reluctance, discomfort.
Ⓒ The Lord's judgment upon an appeal ends the matter with absolute finality. Re-arguing constitutes defiant dissent.
