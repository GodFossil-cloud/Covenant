Primer

Orientation

Before you proceed to the Covenant, you must understand the structure and expectations of this initiation.

The Path Ahead

This initiation consists of five stages, each building upon the last. You will move through them in strict sequence. There are no shortcuts.

What You Will Encounter

  • The Covenant: A written agreement of principles and commitments.
  • Daily Rituals: Structured practices to integrate these principles into your life.
  • The Oath: A spoken, recorded commitment witnessed and signed.
  • Consecration: The final acknowledgment of your transformation.

Requirements

You will need:

  • A quiet space for the Oath recording
  • A device capable of audio recording and signature capture
  • Full presence and undivided attention
  • Willingness to be bound by your word

Proceed with Awareness

This is not a casual undertaking. Once you enter the Covenant, you are moving toward a binding commitment. If you have doubt, return to the threshold. If you are certain, continue forward.
