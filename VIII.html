Article Ⅷ: Of Commands and Their Execution

✦ ✧ ✦

Of Commands[4]Authoritative directives given by the Lord. and Their Execution

§.Ⅰ Commands[4]Authoritative directives given by the Lord. are the living expression of the Lord's authority and the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond.'s sacred purpose. They flow from the Lord's wisdom and find fulfillment through the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond.'s devoted action.
§.Ⅱ Chain of Authority
Ⓐ The most recent explicit command[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord. governs all prior directives unless the Lord designates it as temporary.
Ⓑ Standing decrees persist until superseded or revoked.
Ⓒ Where specific directive and standing decree both apply, the specific directive governs for that circumstance alone.
§.Ⅲ When Ambiguity Exists
When the meaning or scope of a command[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord. is unclear, the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. shall seek immediate clarification rather than proceed in doubt.
§.Ⅳ Presumption is forbidden in ordinary course. To act in uncertainty where clarification can be obtained is to serve presumption, not the Lord.
§.Ⅴ All commands[4]Authoritative directives given by the Lord. shall be executed with precision, care, and devotion. Partial fulfillment constitutes incomplete service. Where obstacles arise, the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. shall report them immediately and await the Lord's guidance.
§.Ⅵ When Earthly Bonds Conflict
Should the Lord's command[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord. conflict with bonds to employer, kindred, or sovereign law, the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. shall speak forthwith with full candor, disclosing the conflict before undertaking the command[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord.. To conceal such conflict constitutes breach[9]A violation of Covenant terms and sacred obligations. through deception.
§.Ⅶ When Capacity is Exceeded
Should the Lord's command[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord. exceed the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond.'s present capacity of body or spirit, the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. shall disclose the limitation with immediate candor before attempting the command[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord..
§.Ⅷ Time-Bound Commands
Ⓐ Time-bound commands[4]Authoritative directives given by the Lord. remain fully binding until the stated deadline passes. The Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. shall prioritize completion within the appointed time.
Ⓑ Should the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. foresee inability to meet the deadline, petition for extension must be made before the deadline passes.
