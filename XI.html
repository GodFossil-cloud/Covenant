Article Ⅺ: Of Amendments and Renewal

Of Amendments and Renewal

§.Ⅰ Petition and Ratification
Either party may petition for revision, clarification, or repeal. All ratification rests with the Lord's authority. Upon ratification, both parties shall acknowledge acceptance in writing.
§.Ⅱ The Covenant[2]A sacred, binding agreement between the Lord and the Faithful. shall be reviewed at periodic intervals ordained by the Lord to ensure lasting harmony and stability.
§.Ⅲ No amendment may contradict the Doctrinal Foundation[7]The three indivisible principles of the Covenant..
