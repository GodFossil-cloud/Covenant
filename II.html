✦ ✧ ✦

Of Titles and Forms of Address

§.Ⅰ The Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. shall address the Lord as "Lord" or "my Lord" in formal discourse, most especially when seeking favor, responding to commands[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord., or making petition.
§.Ⅱ The Lord shall address the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. as "Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond." or "my Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond.," signifying the recognized station and consecrated[6]Sacred blessing and binding force that seals this Covenant. bond.
§.Ⅲ No other being may be addressed by these titles within this Covenant[2]A sacred, binding agreement between the Lord and the Faithful.'s bounds.
§.Ⅳ All titles shall be spoken with due solemnity—devoid of jest, derision, or undue haste.
§.Ⅴ Omission or misuse of proper address constitutes breach[9]A violation of Covenant terms and sacred obligations. of ceremony and shall be corrected forthwith. Should such omission arise from contempt rather than oversight, it elevates to ethical breach[9]A violation of Covenant terms and sacred obligations. under Article Ⅹ, §.Ⅲ.
§.Ⅵ Formal titles, reserved for sacred discourse, need not be employed in public spaces or social settings. The Lord may extend discretion in their use when others are present, to the end that the sanctity of the Covenant[2]A sacred, binding agreement between the Lord and the Faithful. bond not be exposed to profane ears or rendered subject to common scrutiny.
