Declaration of Intent

The Declaration of Intent marks the ceremonial threshold. Here, both Lord and Faithful formally acknowledge their consent, understanding, and acceptance of the sacred bond they are entering.

This section operates as a solemn affirmation, establishing that the Covenant is not coerced but chosen, not casual but consecrated. It closes with a ritual pronouncement: by this Declaration, the Covenant begins.
