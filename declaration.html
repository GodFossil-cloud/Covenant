Declaration of Intent

✦ ✧ ✦

Let it be known and sealed beneath witness: this Covenant[2]A sacred, binding agreement between the Lord and the Faithful. is entered into freely, solemnly, and with full recognition of its gravity. 2 It binds the Lord and the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. in unwavering accord and sacred duty.

3 Both parties, standing at the threshold of consecrated[6]Sacred blessing and binding force that seals this Covenant. bondage, affirm their understanding that this is no ordinary compact but a sacred ordering of will and purpose. 4 By entering herein, each accepts the weight and blessing of the station they shall occupy—the Lord as sovereign guide and righteous commander, the Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. as devoted servant and obedient instrument.

5 Every act and word under this Covenant[2]A sacred, binding agreement between the Lord and the Faithful. shall be answerable to the Foundation[7]The three indivisible principles of the Covenant. above and the structure herein established. 6 Both parties, being of sound mind and clear purpose, declare their full understanding of these terms and their willing submission to this bond for so long as this Covenant[2]A sacred, binding agreement between the Lord and the Faithful. shall endure.

7 By this Declaration, the threshold is crossed. 8 By this Declaration, the Covenant[2]A sacred, binding agreement between the Lord and the Faithful. begins.
