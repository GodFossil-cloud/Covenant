Doctrinal Foundation

✦ ✧ ✦

This Covenant[2]A sacred, binding agreement between the Lord and the Faithful. rests upon three indivisible principles from which all authority derives:

Hierarchy[3]The sacred ordering of authority and obedience. is sacred.

Command[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord. is rightful.

Obedience[5]The willing submission and faithful execution of commands. is consecrated[6]Sacred blessing and binding force that seals this Covenant..

5 The bond between Lord and Faithful[1]The devoted servant and obedient instrument within this sacred bond. draws its authority from this immutable order, freely chosen and solemnly entered. 6 True lordship unites authority with care, command[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord. with nurture, power with wisdom.

7 All Articles that follow operate under this Foundation[7]The three indivisible principles of the Covenant.. 8 Where matters of sanctity, hierarchy[3]The sacred ordering of authority and obedience., obedience[5]The willing submission and faithful execution of commands., or command[4]An authoritative directive given by the Lord. are referenced, they draw their meaning from this eternal Foundation[7]The three indivisible principles of the Covenant., which remains inviolate through all amendments and renewals.

9 From this Foundation[7]The three indivisible principles of the Covenant. flows all authority, obligation, and sacred structure herein established.
